ADDITION

Addition is combining two or more numbers together to get a new total. The symbol used in addition problems looks like a cross, +, and is called a plus sign. The total for addition is called the sum. Other common words used in addition problems are add, plus, combine, together, total, in all, and increase.

Example:

Kuya Jhanel wants to make pizza for dessert.
He looks at the table and sees that he only has 4 pineapples, which isn’t enough.

So, Kuya Jhanel goes to the market to buy more pineapples.
He purchases 2 pineapples from a fruit stand.
How many pineapples does Kuya Jhanel have now?

4 + 2 = 6

Four plus Two is Six

So, Kuya Jhanel now has 6 pineapples!
