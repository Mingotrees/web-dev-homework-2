    SUBTRACTION

    Subtraction is when you take away a number from another to find how much is left. The result of subtracting two or more numbers is called the difference. It is taking away a number or amount from another number or amount. It is the inverse operation of addition.

    Example:

    Kuya Jhanell loves pineapples! He loves eating them for snacks. Currently, his kitchen has 7 pineapples.

    Oh no! Kuya Jhanell’s older brother, Kuya Justin, is feeling hungry! He goes to the kitchen and eats 4 pineapples! How many pineapples does Kuya Jhanell have left?

    Subtraction is like taking away things to see how many are left. For example, if Kuya Jhanell has 7 pineapples and Kuya Justin eats 4, we can find out how many pineapples Kuya Jhanell has left by taking away those 4 pineapples!

    7 - 4 = 3

    Seven minus Four is Three

    So, Kuya Jhanell now has 3 pineapples left!


    Here are more examples!

    6 - 1 = 5

    Six minus One is Five

    8 - 3 = 5

    Eight minus Three is Five

    2 - 1 = 1

    Two minus One is One

    2 - 2 = 0

    Two minus Two is Zero
